# ⛳ Bunch Golf

Arcade-but-real golf on the front nine of **El Macero Country Club** — hole shapes, bunkers, the island-green 7th, and real scorecard yardages (par 36, 3,503 yds from the blues).

**Play it:** https://bunch313131.github.io/bunchgolf/

📱 On iPhone: open the link in Safari → Share → **Add to Home Screen** for the full-screen app with icon.

## How to play
- **Drag back and release** to swing — drag farther for more power, drift sideways for draw/fade
- Keep it at ~80% for a control swing; going all-out gets loose
- The aim line shows a **calm-air** shot — judge the wind yourself (Member/Tour difficulty)
- Tap the minimap to aim, tap a club to override the caddie
- On the green: drag back to putt — the dotted line shows how the break starts

Single file, no install, no accounts. Best round is saved on your device.
